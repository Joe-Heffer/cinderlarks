Book us

If you'd like to book us, license a song or want to discuss a project we'd be happy to have a chat. Get in touch.

We are for fans of alt, indie, folk, rock.

Our music is atmospheric lyrical songs with intricate guitar work and bursts of art-rock.

Think of The Mountain Goats, XTC and Turin Brakes.

We're always on the lookout for festivals, multi-band or support events, ideally with existing backline and engineer.

Our music is all originals, 45 minute long set.

We can be flexible as to how we play to suit different gigs and set-ups, including fully acoustic or intimate venues.
