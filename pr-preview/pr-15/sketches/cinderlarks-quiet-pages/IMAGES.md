# Quiet Pages — image prompts

`index.html` currently renders without any raster images (hero motifs and
song-card art are all inline SVG + CSS gradients, so the sketch stays
dependency-free). These are prompts for the real photography/textures that
would replace those placeholders before this leaves sketch stage. Written for
Midjourney / DALL·E / Stable Diffusion — adjust aspect ratio flags per tool.

Shared style notes for every prompt below: warm cream and dusty-pink light,
desaturated, slightly faded like an old print; documentary/still-life
photography or fine pencil-line illustration, never glossy or hyper-real;
soft window light, no flash; nothing staged-looking. Negative prompt for all:
`no text, no logos, no watermark, not oversaturated, no studio strobe, no
AI-art sheen, no people unless specified`.

## Hero background

**"Open diary on a windowsill"** — A worn leather-bound diary lying open on a
wooden windowsill, blank cream pages slightly rippled with age, a dried
pressed flower resting in the gutter of the book, soft overcast daylight from
the window, shallow depth of field, dust motes visible in the light, muted
sage-green and dusty-pink color grade, 35mm documentary film photography,
Kodak Portra look. Wide aspect ratio (16:9 or 21:9) for a full-bleed hero.

## Background texture

**"Cream linen paper grain"** — Close-up macro photograph of aged cream
linen writing paper, visible fiber texture and faint foxing spots, evenly
lit, no text or marks, desaturated warm neutral tone, tileable/seamless.
Square or small aspect ratio, meant to repeat as a subtle full-page
background at low opacity.

## Song imagery (one image per song, same treatment as the hero)

1. **Gold** — A single tarnished gold ring resting in a small ceramic dish by
   a kitchen sink, warm late-afternoon light catching the metal, water
   droplets nearby, worn wooden windowsill, shallow depth of field,
   documentary still-life photography, warm gold and dusty-pink tones.

2. **Cups and Rings** — A chipped ceramic jug and matching cup on a wooden
   windowsill, faint white water-ring stains on the wood beneath them, soft
   natural daylight, muted sage-green and cream palette, documentary
   still-life photography.

3. **She Wrote Me a Letter** — A ribbon-tied bundle of envelopes resting on
   an old hall table beside a fountain pen, one envelope on top left
   unopened, soft window light, aged cream paper tones with a touch of dusty
   pink from the ribbon, documentary still-life photography.

4. **Pocket Full of Fossils** — Small ammonite fossil spirals spilling out of
   the pocket of a worn wool coat draped over a chair, alongside a bus ticket
   and loose change, soft indoor light, warm neutral tones, documentary
   still-life photography, macro-ish framing.

5. **Where Did All the Time Go** — A small antique mantel clock on a dusty
   windowsill, hands used sparingly (faint, not centered), soft late-day
   light through net curtains, warm gold accent against a muted sage
   background, documentary still-life photography.

## Band photography (for About / footer, if used)

**"Four-piece, rehearsal room"** — A documentary-style band photograph of
four musicians in a modest rehearsal room above an old shop, natural window
light, instruments visible but not posed formally, candid mid-conversation
moment rather than a lineup stare, warm desaturated color grade matching the
site's cream/pink/sage palette, 35mm film photography look, shot from a
slight distance so the room (bare walls, a kettle, a spare guitar case) reads
as clearly as the people.

## Icon motifs (optional illustrated variants)

If replacing the inline-SVG line icons with illustrated/painterly versions,
use: **"Single-object botanical/still-life illustration, fine ink line with
a light watercolor wash in dusty pink, sage green or faded gold, on cream
paper, no shading gradients, no background, centered composition"** — applied
individually to: a wedding-band ring, a chipped water jug, a sealed envelope,
an ammonite fossil spiral, a mantel clock face.
