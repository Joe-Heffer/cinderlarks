We are for fans of alt, indie, folk, rock. Our music is atmospheric lyrical songs with intricate guitar work and bursts of art-rock.

Our music is all originals, 45 minute long set. We're always on the lookout for festivals, multi-band or support events, ideally with existing backline and engineer. We can be flexible as to how we play to suit different gigs and set-ups, including fully acoustic or intimate venues.

Think of The Mountain Goats, XTC and Turin Brakes.
