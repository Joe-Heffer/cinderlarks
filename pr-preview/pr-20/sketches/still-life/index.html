The Cinderlarks

Enigmatic melodies of loss and hope, played soft — Sheffield folk-rock for grandmothers’ rings and unopened letters.

listen closer

“Soft songs for old rooms — memory, grief and tenderness, played slow.”

The Cinderlarks are a Sheffield four-piece — Joe C on vocals, Rich on guitar, Joe H on bass and Greg on drums — writing soft dad-rock threaded with folk and country: closer to The Smiths’ melancholy and The Beatles’ melodic craft than anything loud.
