Cinderlarks — Website Concept Sketches

Prototype designs, each in its own folder under sketches/.

  • cinderlarks-quiet-pages — literary "quiet pages" concept: songs as chapters, cream/pastel palette, typographic songs index with hover-reveal motifs
  • drowned — "The Kid Who Drowned" song page: a darker, submerged variant of the site's palette for the album's heaviest song
  • rings-and-cups — an alternate "Cups and Rings" concept: Neolithic cup-and-ring stone carvings, weathered grey/moss/ochre palette with a single blood-red accent, hand-carved typography and generous negative space
  • template — starting point for a new sketch, not a real design
