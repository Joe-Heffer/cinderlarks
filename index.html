Cinderlarks — Website Concept Sketches

Prototype designs, each in its own folder under sketches/.

  • cinderlarks-quiet-pages — literary "quiet pages" concept, now a single-page Tailwind hero landing (Playfair Display/Lora/Caveat, cream/dusty-pink/sage/gold palette) with About, Songs, Shows and Contact as scrolling chapters, plus five full song "chapter" pages to turn to for the deeper read
  • drowned — "The Kid Who Drowned" song page: a darker, submerged variant of the site's palette for the album's heaviest song
  • rings-and-cups — an alternate "Cups and Rings" concept: Neolithic cup-and-ring stone carvings, weathered grey/moss/ochre palette with a single blood-red accent, hand-carved typography and generous negative space
  • steel-and-static — a general band landing/hero page in an "industrial / analogue grit" aesthetic: Sheffield steel-city textures, rust and brass tones, and DIY tape/screen-print motifs as a bold backdrop for a still-gentle folk band
  • still-life — a single-page Tailwind hero prototype: cream/dusty-pink/sage/plum "quiet pages" palette, Fraunces/Public Sans/Caveat type, hero treated as a still life of hand-drawn song motifs (ring, jug, envelope, fossil, clock) instead of a band photo
  • template — starting point for a new sketch, not a real design
